Reading Group: Applied Toric Geometry

Together with Yue Jiao and Lucy Jiang, we are organizing a reading group about Simon Telen's book-in-progress Applied Toric Varieties.

The reading group is open to all interested students and researchers. The aim is to read Chpaters 1-5 and 7-9, discuss the content and solve exercises.

In case of questions, please write an mail to any of us: Angelo El Saliby, Yue Jiao or Lucy Jiang.

Textbook

Schedule

We will meet at lest once a week, on Wednesday at 2PM, for a total of six weeks.

The reading group will be held online, a Zoom link will be communicated via mail.

For each session, one or two session chairs will be assigned. They will make a summary of the assigned chapters and lead the discussion. Each session should last roughly 90 minutes.
